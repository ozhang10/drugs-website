Hepatitis B

Pathophysiology and Impact

This is a paragraph about medications specific to Hepatitis B. The complications resulting from Hepatitis B infections vary from mild illness to chronic infection leading to

  • cirrhosis
  • hepatocarcinoma
  • liver failure

and ultimately, it may result in death. Prevention & treatment are of utmost importance in reducing mortality and morbidity.
